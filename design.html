What makes it different
01
Your roommate
will never know.

Brushless motor, under 35dB. Quieter than a conversation. Quieter than the TV. Quieter than you.

02
Looks like
an earbud case.

The form references a familiar object. No one has ever guessed what it actually is. That's the point. That's the whole point.

03
No buttons.
No thinking.

Swipe the surface to change intensity. Press to turn on or off. There is no step three. No modes, no Bluetooth, no app, no problem.

04
Take it in
the shower.

IPX7. One metre of water for thirty minutes. We don't ask questions. Flush USB-C port so there's nothing to get gross or break.

05

104mm long. 28mm thick. Goes in a pocket, a purse, a weekender bag. Doesn't look weird going through security. Has never looked weird going through security.
