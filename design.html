SOLA

Touch-controlled. Pocket-sized. Nobody knows what it is.

Explore the Design
Why it works

Designed to disappear.
Built to deliver.

Every decision — form, material, input — serves one principle: it should be completely unrecognizable for what it is.

Touch-Only Control

Zero buttons. The entire top surface is capacitive. Swipe up for intensity, swipe down to ease off, press to toggle. No instruction manual needed.

Pebble Form Factor

104 × 78 × 28mm. Fits closed in a fist. Sits on a shelf like a worry stone. No one looks twice. That's the whole point.

Matte Silicone

Body-safe Shore A 40 silicone. Soft-touch matte finish. IPX7 waterproof. No visible seams, no hard edges, no cheap plastic. Feels premium because it is.

Whisper Motor

Brushless motor under 35dB. Quieter than a conversation. You'll feel it, no one else will hear it. Engineered for discretion at every intensity.

Flush USB-C

Recessed charging port with no cap, no cover, no protrusion. Plugs in, charges up, nothing ruins the silhouette. 90 minutes of use per charge.

Nothing to Explain

No app required. No pairing. No modes to memorize. Pick it up and use it. Complexity is the enemy of intimacy.
