Design Principles

Disguise

The form references familiar everyday objects. No feature — shape, port, texture — should reveal its function to an uninformed observer.

Touch-first

Zero buttons. The entire top face is a capacitive touch surface. Swipe controls intensity. A firm press toggles power. Intuitive without instruction.

Shelf-worthy

Should look at home next to skincare and ceramics. No overtly sexual curves or branding. Muted palette. Minimal embossed mark only.

No apology

The brand doesn't hide what it is — the product does. Honest, direct, a little bold. Not pink, not clinical. Has an edge.
