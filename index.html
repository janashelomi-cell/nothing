HOW IT
WORKS
01
Sign up — it's free

Drop your email. We'll send a welcome message and a link to the group chat. No credit card, no commitment.

02
Vote on next month's pick

Members nominate and vote at the end of each meeting. Every genre welcome, every voice counts.

03
Read at your pace

No deadlines. Optional weekly check-ins in the group chat if you want the discourse mid-book.

Last Saturday of the month. In person or on Zoom. Come for the book, stay for the opinions.
