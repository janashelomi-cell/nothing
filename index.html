How it works

Simple as
turning a page.

1
Join the club

Sign up with your email. It's free. You'll get a welcome message and an invite to the members group chat.

2
Vote on next month's pick

Members nominate and vote at the end of each meeting. Every voice counts, every genre is welcome.

3
Read at your own pace

No deadlines, no pressure. We share short weekly check-ins in the chat for those who want them.

4
Come to the meeting

Last Saturday of the month, in person or on Zoom. Come for the books, stay for the conversation.
